Sleep Tips

8 hours of sleep â‰  8 hours of bedtime

At least 8 hours of sleep is important for muscle growth but you need to be in bed one hour before so you will have enough time to fall asleep and get 8 hours of deep sleep. Therefore, you need to set 9 hours of bedtime to get 8 hours of sleep. Bedtime is the time duration you spend in bed to fall asleep. In bedtime, you should forget everything else and focus on falling asleep alone, you can do that by focusing on breathing deep.

Spend your energy throughout the day

Spending energy throughout the day, through activities such as exercise, work, or even mental stimulation, can help promote better sleep at night by increasing feelings of tiredness and promoting relaxation.

Focus on your breathing and meditate until you fall asleep

Focusing on deep breathing while lying in bed can help relax the body and mind, making it easier to fall asleep. Deep breathing slows the heart rate, reduces muscle tension, and promotes feelings of relaxation. Simply inhale slowly through your nose and exhale slowly through your mouth, focusing on expanding your belly and releasing tension with each breath.

Keep your sleep environment dark, cool, and comfy

Creating a dark, cool, and comfortable sleep environment can promote better sleep by helping the body produce melatonin, facilitating the natural drop in body temperature, and reducing discomfort and stress. Make sure the bedroom is dark, keep the temperature cool (around 60-67 degrees Fahrenheit), and use comfortable bedding.
