Sleep Tips

Sleep is an essential factor in hypertrophy training, as it plays a crucial role in muscle recovery and growth. During sleep, the body releases growth hormone, which is essential for building and repairing muscle tissue. Additionally, sleep is also when the body goes through the process of muscle protein synthesis, which is the process of repairing and building muscle tissue.
